# Company Profile: SaaSFlow

## Overview
SaaSFlow is a leading cloud-based project management platform designed for high-growth startups and agencies. We help teams consolidate their tasks, documentation, and team communication into a single, automated workspace.

## Mission
To empower teams to eliminate "work about work" through intelligent automation and intuitive design.

## Target Audience
- **Startups**: Rapidly scaling engineering teams.
- **Agencies**: Managing multiple client workflows.
- **Enterprise**: Large departments requiring high-level visibility.

## Support Strategy
Our AI-first support model ensures customers get technical help 24/7. Routine queries are handled by Flowie (our Digital FTE), while high-complexity cases are escalated to human specialists.
